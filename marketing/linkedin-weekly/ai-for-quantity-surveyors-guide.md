## Post 2 — Hook / time-saver (attach ai-for-quantity-surveyors-guide-hook.png)

"Will AI replace quantity surveyors?"

No. It cannot measure off a drawing, set a commercial rate, or decide entitlement, and it has no accountability for the numbers it produces.

What it can do is take a day of tender comparison and turn it into an hour.

Paste three pricing schedules and the qualification pages into Claude. Ask it to separate prelims, normalise to a common scope, list every exclusion, and flag outliers.

A couple of minutes later you are not staring at three incomparable totals. You are looking at the three commercial questions that actually matter.

You still make the call. You just skipped the spreadsheet.
