## UK vs US MARKET COMPARISON

### Key Differences

| Metric | UK | US |
|--------|-----|-----|
| Seed keywords total SV | 4,800 | 5,100 |
| Matching terms found | 1,385 | 1,219 |
| Matching terms total SV | 17,000 | 21,000 |
| Matching terms total GSV | 45,000 | 46,000 |
| Biggest keyword | method statement template (800) | ai in construction (2,000) |
| Template keyword volume | **Much higher** (RAMS 700, method stmt 800) | Lower (different terminology) |
| AI keyword volume | Lower (ai in construction 500) | **Much higher** (ai in construction 2,000) |
| Construction estimating AI | 50 | **300** ($7.00 CPC!) |

### Strategic Implications

1. **UK is your template market** — UK construction professionals search for RAMS, method statements, construction phase plans at HIGH volumes with KD 0-1. These terms barely register in the US.

2. **US is your AI content market** — "ai in construction" (2,000 SV), "ai construction estimating" (300 SV, $7 CPC), "ai for construction" (500 SV) are all much bigger in the US.

3. **BuildCopilot should target BOTH** — UK templates drive initial traffic; US AI keywords drive growth. The site naturally serves both with different page types.

4. **US terminology differs** — Americans search for "construction daily report" not "site diary", "OSHA toolbox talks" not "toolbox talks", "estimates" not "tender evaluation". Consider creating US-friendly page variants or using both terms.

5. **UK-specific terms are a moat** — RAMS, CDM, JCT, NEC, golden thread, construction phase plan, CIOB — these UK-specific terms have zero US competition. Own them completely.
